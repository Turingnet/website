Trustable Artificial Intelligence
Mission
Create an AI ecosystem to serve the best interest of community
so that everyone is able to leverage and benefit from AI technologies.
Why
AI Models are controlled by centralized owners
Models in a centralized AI system often owned by tech giants like Google, Amazon, etc. These models and the processes of training and inferencing are only accessible internally. This problem is one of the biggest concerns of AI from general public.
Waste and imbalance of computation powers
Typical AI system requires massive computation powers in model training and inference. Multiple centralized systems have built their own data centers but not shared to each other, which lead to enormous resource waste. There is a great opportunity to organize computation powers from public in a decentralized but trustable way.
Public is not incentivized to train and use AI
General public cannot participate in AI. One of the major reasons is that they are not incentivized on centralized AI systems. Owners of the centralized AI systems are not obligated to pay people who want to contribute their computation powers, as they all possess large data centers with much lower cost per unit power. The ecosystem then separates the public from these big companies, who further strengthens their owned centralized AI systems and models.
High operational cost
Centralized AI systems are operated by different owners separately. Although the cost per unit power is lower, the scale of these systems often lead to enormous cost to run. Such high cost may pressure the owners to perform malicious business activities for maximizing profits.
Data privacy
Training and optimizing AI models require large amount of data. The primary sources of such training data are from user characteristics and interactions, such as genome, faces, user behaviors on clicking ads, etc.. These private data are highly sensitive. The black box of a centralized AI system has no audition on how the data are being used.
Technology
TuringNet creates a new blockchain infrastructure to power trustable AI services, where model is trained collaboratively with decentralized data and computing resources.
Decentralized Machine Learning
At beginning, we will run the system on mining machines. In the future, we will shift to computers used in people’s daily life, machines are used in people’s home, office and self driving car.
PoL-BFT Consensus
TuringNet will create a completely unique consensus “proof-of-learning” PoL-BFT which is specific for AI blockchain that will reward each participant for the learning work done via data and computing resource contribution.
TuringNet Token
To incentivize key stakeholders, TuringNet will be issuing a digital currency called TuringNet Token (TNT) on the blockchain operated by smart contract. The TNT tokens will be used in multiple ways within the TuringNet ecosystem.
