Decentralized Artificial Intelligence
Mission
Create an AI ecosystem to serve the best interest of community
so that everyone is able to leverage and benefit from AI technologies.
Why AI?
The development of artificial intelligence (AI) is no longer theoretic: machines that think for themselves are no longer confined to sci-fi thrillers, they are in our living room and giving us recommendations on what to make for dinner. We are officially living in the era of artificial intelligence and machine learning, and the question is no longer whether or not artificial intelligence will infiltrate our society, the question is how we will succeed or fail at controlling it now that it has.
Why Decentralized?
In our world, 20% people controlled 80% capital. With advancing of technologies, people with capital will control all aspects of others life. It’s the time to for technology democracy revolution.Your life is controlled by Centralized Recommendation System, companies who own these platform make hundred billion dollars with your transaction and your data. With DAI, you will own a share of the system. Everyone will have the right to decide how to use the data. Everyone will get financial reward by sharing the data.
Why BlockChain?
Blockchain is a natural decentralized system. Everyone who own coins has the vote right. Blockchain is a the weapon for 80% people to end the rule of 20% people.What more, the current mining wastes vast amounts of energy, and don’t create any real value, except the virtual money. With our system, we will turn the mining machines into computer resource for deep learning, the core technology of artificial intelligence.The blockchain is going to provide a lot of opportunities for AI.
