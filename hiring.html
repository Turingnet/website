Senior Software Engineer - Large-scale blockchain Infrastructure

Turingnet is seeking an experienced Senior Software Engineer - Blockchain Infrastructure to join the Engineering team. Our large scale infrastructure serves all Turingnet products. We handle everything from serving traffic across client nodes and server nodes, to unified tensorflow job workloads, etc. A Blockchain Infrastructure Engineer is someone who is extremely knowledgeable about the large scale system, has experiencing developing microservices on top of it (preferably blockchain stack), and is passionate about building innovative solutions for the world's leading institutions. You should be knowledgeable of the existing large scale system stack and visionary in your approach to leveraging its components. You should have a background in architecting large scale web service and server systems. You will see projects through from technical strategy and wireframes, to the nitty gritty implementation.
Responsibilities
Working with a team of expert developers to develop large-scale blockchain-based infrastructure and decentralized applications for individual developer or enterprise use;
Leading the development of infrastructure of online marketplace web service;
Establishing and maintaining the environments in which blockchain solutions can be implemented, including development, testing, and production environments;Maintain a continuous integration pipeline for the development and testing of applications;
Educate clients and internal development teams on the blockchain-related elements of the stack;
You will be required from time to time to work in other areas of the team (front-end development, back-end development, etc);
Conduct design and code reviews;
Analyze and improve efficiency, scalability, and stability of various system resources;
Design core, backend or frontend software components.
Requirements
3+ years of relevant industry coding experience in C, C++, Java;
3+ years of industry expertise experience of distributed system;
3+ years relevant experience building large scale server infrastructure, or data pipeline;
Master or PhD in Computer Science or related technical field;
Experience working with product team on both client and server side;
Experience working on front end frameworks.
